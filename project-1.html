Where the Folk Poster

Poster for a fictional music festival “Where the Folk?”, a small-scale folk-festival-meets-treasure-hunt, with each poster inspired by a different outdoor concert venue.

  • About

    As a passionate illustrator and occasional woodland sorcerer, I’m ready to conquer any creative adventure thrown my way. I’m enchanted by the little things, which comes in handy, because I love tinkering until I get the details just right. When I’m not conjuring fantasy worlds with my work, I’m bringing them to life in my weekly DND games—which I never miss.
